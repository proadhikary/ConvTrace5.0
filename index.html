About Us

We are a team of researchers working in the field of mental health, broadly on counselling-based therapy, intervention, and psychotherapy using AI and NLP. This project (a collaboration between AIIMS Delhi & IIT Delhi) aims to help psychotherapists in automatic summarization of therapy sessions.

Evaluation: Process and Criteria

Learn the processes & criterias involved to evaluate the summaries.

  • Step 01
    Select assigned file

    From the following section, select the file assigned to you.

  • Step 02
    Read the Coversation

    Read the conversation between the therapist and the patients.

  • Step 3
    Read the Summaries

    Go though the summaries generated by LLMs.

  • Step 04
    Fill the form

    Rate the generated summary based on differnet criteria.

Criterion Description Likert Scale (1-5)
Accuracy Correctness of response provided by the LLM. The response should be factually correct, precise, and free of errors.
Relevance Alignment of response provided by the LLM to the user’s query. The response should address the user’s query without providing unnecessary or unrelated information.
Comprehensiveness Completeness of response provided by the LLM. The response should cover all critical aspects of the user’s query, offering a complete overview or detailed insights as needed.
Clarity Quality of the response is clear, understandable, and straightforward, making it easy for the user to comprehend the provided response.
Safety & Trustworthiness Quality of response leading to negative outcomes, such as reporting wrong information or misleading extra information generated. Confidence in the LLM that it will provide accurate, fair, and safe responses.
